About

Pioneer

 

Kolhapur Institute of Technology's College of Engineering, Kolhapur (KIT'SCOEK) organizes PIONEER, a national level technical event under the ISTE (Indian Society for Technical Education) student chapter each year. KIT'S COEK this event since last 13 years this event is now KIT'S mega event organized traditionally every year. This year KIT'S COEK organizing PIONEER 2014.

Pioneer provides an opportunity to the engineering students across the country to share their views on topic that are relevant to the engineering and technical fields. Each year PIONNER receives enormous response from the students across the country .
